Play on Windows/Mac

Install the Flash Player for Online Play

You can install/update the Flash Player plugin for your web browser online at:
http://get.adobe.com/flashplayer/
Then you can play Flash games online through your web browser.

Flash Player Projector for Offline Play

You can also download the Flash Player Projector - the standalone executable player for playing '.swf' files offline/locally:
http://www.adobe.com/support/flashplayer/downloads.html


Play on Android

Install the Flash Player for Online Play

Firstly, you need a web browser supporting Flash, for example, FireFox for Android:
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.mozilla.firefox
Or the lightweight Now Browser:
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.browser.nowbasic
Then follow the tutorials below to install Flash Player for your Android web browser:
http://www.androidpit.com/how-to-install-flash-player-on-android
http://www.androidpit.com/how-to-install-flash-player-on-android-4-4-kitkat
http://www.greenbot.com/article/2142627/how-to-hack-flash-support-onto-your-modern-android-phone.html

Flash/SWF Viewer for Online Play

After you install the official Flash Player for Android, you can also install an unofficial Flash/SWF Viewer to play flash games offline/locally:
https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.issess.flashplayer


Play on Linux

Install the Flash Player 11.2 Final For Linux:
http://get.adobe.com/cn/flashplayer/otherversions/
Or use Chrome for Linux:
http://www.google.com/chrome/intl/en/w00t.html


Play on IOS

Install the Puffin Browser for your iPad or iPhone:
http://www.puffinbrowser.com/download/
You can enable the build-in theater mode and gamepad when playing Flash games.
