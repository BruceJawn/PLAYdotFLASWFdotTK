Street Fighter II - Champion Edition, is finally back to your web browser announcing the launch of Street Figther IV!

Pick one of the 12 characters available in this Champion Edition: Ryu, Ken, Honda, Chun-li, Blanka, Guile, Zangief, Dhalsim, Balrog, Vega, Sagat and M.Bison. After every third match you will participate in a "bonus game" including car-breaking, barrel breaking and drum-breaking for additional points.

Will you survive till the end by defeating each opponent? This version is only available in single player mode but who knows what might happen if YOU make it big!
