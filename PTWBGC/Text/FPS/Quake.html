[Introduction]
The original Quake is a first-person shooter released by id Software in 1996. Quake Flash was ported by Michael Rennie. After the game loaded, press SPACEBAR and select SINGLE PLAYER, then press Enter to start.

[Controls]
View: Left Click and Drag
Move: ARROW KEYS(holding SHIFT will speed your movement)
Switch weapons: NUMBER KEYS or /
Move left or right: A and D KEYS
Jump: SPACEBAR or Enter
Fire: CTRL
