[Introduction]
Arcane online mystery serial. In the game you try to lead an inquiry and elucidate a few riddles. To properly play this game, you must thoroughly explore each place that you will visit. Scan the game screen with your cursor. By moving it from left to right, sound or visual clues will lead you towards another room or another place. You must locate the objects that will help you solve the riddles. Those objects will be picked up by your character and will thereafter appear in the "inventory" at the bottom of the game screen, you will be permitted to use those objects at your convenience. Time runs out with each clicking on the mouse.

[Controls]
Mouse click to move in the chosen direction and interact with the objects.
