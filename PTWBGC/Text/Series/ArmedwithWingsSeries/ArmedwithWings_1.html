[Introduction]
Travel through the lands, in a quest to bring justice to the one who destroyed the rebellion army. Armed with an eagle, solve puzzles and beat down bad guys in this action packed plaformer. If you encounter any glitches while playing, hit the restart level button at the top right side.

[Controls]
Avatar
Move: Left Right
Attack: Z
Block: X

Eagle
Release eagle: A
Pickup items: S
Keep eagle idle: D
