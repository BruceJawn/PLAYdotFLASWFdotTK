[Introduction]
A Counter-Strike like 3D First Person Shooter based on id Software's quake 1 engine.
A gang of terrorists lurks in an abandoned train station, just waiting for you to board.

[Controls]
Move: W, A, S, D
Aim: Arrow Keys
Change Weapon: 1-3
R: Reload
E: Jump
Shoot: Spacebar
