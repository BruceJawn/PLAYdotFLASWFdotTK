[Introduction]
The renegade stealth assassin is back to take down the evil empire set on creating a deadly mutant weapon. We take over where we left off in the first game where we discover a strange being and intel on world domination. In this game we have new stealth attacks, a suspensful atmosphere and new costumes you can unlock.

[Controls]
Mouse to move, interact with targets or pick up objects.
Press Spacebar to cancel attack.
Press "G" to wear your thermal goggles on keypads.
W, A, S, D keys for stealth attacks.
