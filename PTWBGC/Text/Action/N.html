[Introduction]
For information on rules, how to play, etc., view the In-game Help and Story sections (accessible from the main menu) Check it out! There are a lot of different moves to master.

[Controls]
Jump: Shift
Accelerate Left/Right: Left/Right Arrow
Kill Yourself (useful if you get stuck or trapped, or just for fun): K
Pause game/Quit to main menu: P
Boss Mode: Tab
