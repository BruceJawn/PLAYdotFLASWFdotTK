Hit the rooftops and take a leap of Faith with Mirror's Edge 2D. Enjoy the hand-crafted animation in this smooth platform game developed by Brad Borne, creator of Fancy Pants Adventure.

[Controls]
Run: Left and Right Arrows
Horizontal wall run, vertical wall climb: Up arrow
Duck: Down Arrow
Pause: Space Bar
Jump: S or /
Toggle music: M
Reset lap: R at pause screen
