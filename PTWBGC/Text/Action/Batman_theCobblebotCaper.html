[Introduction]
Gotham city is under attack and only one person can save it, Batman. If you want to be the savior of Gotham City play Batman - The Cobblebot Caper. In the start, Gotham bank is under attack and you have to reach their and save the money from being stolen. You encounter various crooks and penguins sidekicks during your quest. You have to get the money back to Gotham Bank and finish the Boss. Batman is the most amazing action figure for kids. You can also enjoy the lifestyle of Batman by playing Batman - The Cobblebot Caper. This game provides you with different scenarios and villains. The difficulty starts increasing with the increase in levels. You can beat your enemies with the help of Batrang and kicks. So if you think that you are ready for such an awesome adventure play Batman - The Cobblebot Caper.
