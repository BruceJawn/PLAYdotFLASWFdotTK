[Introduction]
A flash game based on the PC S.T.A.L.K.E.R. game. Can you make it pass your enemies alive? S.T.A.L.K.E.R. Flashed is the flash game version of the PC counterpart "S.T.A.L.K.E.R. Shadow of Chernobyl"; a first person shooter game released for the PC and developed by a Ukrainian game developer called GSC Game World. The game is centered on an amnesiac known as "Stalker", an illegal artifact scavenger and explorer who is caught up in the events unfolding in the Ukraine after a second nuclear disaster at Chernobyl. This flash game version is not an FPS but it is a third person action-adventure with some RPG elements to it.

[Controls]
Use arrow keys to move.
Space bar to use weapon.
Mouse to aim and shoot.
1-3 to change weapon.
