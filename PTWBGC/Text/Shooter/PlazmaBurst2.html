[Introduction]
Plazma Burst is a tactical slidescrolling shooter free online game, developed by Eric Gurt with support of Coolbuddy.com released in March 2011.
The player takes on the role of a Marine, which didn't get sent to past in time to change the history to a better life. In Plazma Burst: Forward to the past, after when our hero's ally was critically damaged in the previous game the marine travels back to the Earth to try traveling back in time once again... Will fortune smile to him this time? Try to find the time machine to prevent the coming of thousands of alien usurpers... If its not too late.
